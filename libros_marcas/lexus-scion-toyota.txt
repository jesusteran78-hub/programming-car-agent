Fun Facts to Remember for Code Series 0 001-5000, Long Version

1 Alltumblers are numbered, but are reverse of normal depths, a number 5 is a shallowe cut and anumber 1 depth is the
deepest cut.

2 Tumbler position 8R is always a number 4 depth for the master key, and a number 2 depth for a valet key. This is the only
difference between a master and a valet key.

3 Tumblers 4L & 8L are not used in any locks, but must follow MACS. (So the key works smoothly)

Because of the tolerances of the center groove, you cannot have a number 5 depth on the left track opposite a number 4
or 5 depth. There wouldn't be enough room for the key to pass down in the keyway of the lock (and visa-versa)

5 There is never a number 1 depth in any position but the 4th position from the bow, if there is one at all, Either 4L or 4k.

DECK LID RELEASE NOTES:

ES250

Manual release lever near left side of driver's seat. If trunk lock is double-locked, then check to see if the fold-down
rear seats are locked. If fold-down rear seats are locked then remove the lower rear cushion. Next remove the bolt
that holds in either the left or right side cushion on the upper rear seat to gain access to release the locking lever on
back of the seat in order to fold it down.

ES300 1993-1995

Push in button on drivers door panel, on/off switch located in glove box. If the alarm has been tripped you will have
to reset the alarm. To do this, remove either door panel; follow the two or three wires off of the door cylinder to
where they meet at the snap-connector. Unsnap the connector and jump the leads until the alarm resets. Usually
you cannot hear when the alarm resets. Now you will be able to activate the electric trunk release switch.

GS300, LS300 1990-94, SC300 & SC400

Flip-button and valet key lockout switch located on left lower side of dash panel. By lowering the panel you can
reset the valet lock by pushing in on the detent on the side of the lock housing, this detent may be hidden under a
large wire clip that encircles the housing of the lock. If the alarm has been tripped you will have to reset the alarm.
To do this, remove either door panel; follow the two or three wires from the door cylinder to where they meet at the
snap-connector. Unsnap the connector and jump the leads until the alarm resets. Usually you cannot hear when the
alarm resets. Now you will be able to activate the electric trunk release switch.

LS400-1995-1997

Flip-button and valet key lockout switch located on left lower side of dash panel. By lowering the panel, you can
reset the valet lock by pushing in on the detent on the side of the lock housing. This detent may be hidden under a
large wire clip that encircles the housing of the lock. If the alarm has been tripped you will have to reset the alarm.
To do this, remove the driver's door panel; follow the two or three wires from the door cylinder to where they meet
at the snap-connector. With the door in the open positions, 1) lock the latch mechanism 2) push the inside door
lock button to the locked position, 3) Now cross the wires coming off of the lock cylinder. These steps are important
because Lexus has installed a door lock control unit directly on the door itself and it is necessary to fool the
computer.

TROUBLE SHOOTING

The keys are locked in the trunk.

You unlock the car, but the Valet-Lockout cylinder i is locked. You will have to get to the back of the Valet-Lockout
cylinder by lowering the bottom portion of the driver’s side dash. On the backside of the cylinder is a wire clip that
partially wraps around the cylinder. Remove the clip and depress the plastic retainer, the cylinder will then pop out
to the unlocked position.

The Valet-Lockout cylinder is unlocked, but the trunk does not open

The reason for this is the factory alarm system has shut down certain operations of the on-board computer. If you
close both doors the alarm will re-lock them. To turn the computer back on, you will have to reset the alarm. To do
this, remove either door panel; follow the two or three wires from the door cylinder to where they meet at the snap
connector. Unsnap the connector and jump the leads until the alarm resets. Usually you cannot hear when the alarm
resets. Now you will be able to activate the electric trunk release switch.
